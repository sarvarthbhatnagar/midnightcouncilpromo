The twist

Hidden objectives and earned powers

Roles are only the opening move. Midnight Council layers a private mission on top: a hidden objective only you can see. Complete it and you unlock a power that can torch a vote, slip past daylight pressure, or steal one more breath of night. The table still looks civil until someone cashes in and the exile math snaps sideways.

  • Personal agenda. Everyone chases a side mission that rewards gutsy reads, careful timing, and ruthless patience.
  • Powers on demand. Finish the objective and you earn leverage that can reshape a single round. Exact tools stay secret so paranoia stays honest.
  • Spicy council politics. You are not only hunting Mob and Town lines. You are juggling a clock only you can hear. One quiet completion can turn a losing day into a theatrical comeback.

Learn the rhythm

Short sessions, sharp moments. Here is the promotional tour: enough to hook newcomers, not enough to spoil a master player.

How a match begins

Someone initiates a room and shares a short code. Players join, pick aliases, and when the group is ready the ritual starts. Everyone receives a hidden role.

Night and Daybreak

Night is for secret actions and whispered coordination. Daybreak is for debate, pressure, and exile votes that can reshape the whole board.

Win conditions

The Mob wins when their numbers meet a parity threat against the Town. The Town wins by eliminating the Mob threat. Every vote is a step toward one of those endings.

Play like a show

Lean into performances, timed accusations, and dramatic reversals. The app is the stage. Your friends are the cast.

Designed for groups

Best around a table, great on a voice call, lethal in a group chat with second screens. If you can talk, you can play.

Spoiler-safe rules FAQ ⌄

Do I need accounts? Jump in quickly with lightweight session identity so the focus stays on the room, not the paperwork.

Is every role always in play? Role counts scale with player count so smaller tables stay balanced while bigger tables get more tension.

What if someone is new? Start them as Loyalist energy: watch votes, ask questions, and learn the tells. The app teaches the rest through play.
